About Me

I have over 12 years of experience in software development, focusing on creating scalable, secure, and sustainable web applications. I work primarily with Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Sidekiq, and Svelte, in addition to managing infrastructure on AWS (EC2, S3, RDS, CloudWatch).

I have solid experience in API architecture, integrations with external services, background jobs, deployment automation, and observability (monitoring and metrics). In my workflow, I prioritize automated testing (RSpec), rigorous code review, CI/CD (GitHub Actions and AWS pipelines), and best practices for versioning and security.

One of the most relevant projects was at Santander, where I modernized a legacy cash flow control system — originally in Microsoft Access — transforming it into an integrated, lightweight, and highly reliable web application. The project increased operational productivity and reduced failures in manual processes.

Currently, I am dedicated to backend engineering with Rails, focusing on scalability, automation, and system integration, always seeking to balance technical quality, delivery speed, and product impact.
